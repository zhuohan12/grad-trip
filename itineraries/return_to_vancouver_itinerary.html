Field notes & logistics

Ferry plan — Target the published 9:35 AM Kingston departure; use 10:20 AM as the fallback. Kingston–Edmonds is first-come, first-served for vehicles, and a prepaid ticket does not reserve space. Recheck the WSDOT schedule and alerts before leaving Port Angeles.

Kingston queue — The summer boarding-pass system runs daily from 8 AM to 8 PM. Follow the SR 104 ferry signal and do not approach the tollbooth without the pass when the system is active.

Border waits — Compare Peace Arch and Pacific Highway shortly before Blaine using WSDOT crossing information and CBSA live waits.

Documents — Keep passports or approved travel documents, driving licences, the rental agreement, and proof of insurance within reach. Declare purchases and all food, plant, animal, alcohol, tobacco, and vaping products.

Navigation — Use the Google Maps link for the default Peace Arch route, then change the final crossing only if the live comparison favours Pacific Highway.

Timing rule — If you take the 10:20 AM fallback ferry, shift the I-5, Bellingham, border, and Vancouver estimates 45 minutes later. No other replanning is needed.

Check-in — The target arrival is around 2:30 PM and access begins at 4:00 PM at 854 East 23rd Avenue. If ferry or border delays build, message the host once the revised arrival is clear.
