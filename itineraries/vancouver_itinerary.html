Field notes & logistics

Notion anchors — The Canada Trip 2026 page confirms the staggered AC020 arrivals, July 19–21 Airbnb, and July 21 rental handover.

Luggage — Obtain written host approval for a morning bag drop. Check-in is 4:00 PM; pre-booked paid storage is the fallback, not carrying luggage through Stanley Park.

World Cup Final — Granville Island's closing watch party is on July 19 and can reach its 1,000-person capacity. Keep Granville Island on Monday.

Stanley Park — The park is generally open 6:00 AM–10:00 PM. There is no transit around the park; use the short sampler and check the City of Vancouver park page for alerts.

Transit — Adult contactless fare is C$3.50 from July 1, 2026 and lasts 90 minutes. Sunday travel is one zone; a DayPass is unlikely to beat pay-as-you-go here. Check Trip Planner before each departure.

Granville hours — Public Market summer hours are 9:00 AM–6:00 PM Monday–Wednesday; individual vendors vary. Recheck the official visitor information for changes.

Weather rule — Stanley Park is the weather-dependent block. In heavy rain, shorten it rather than moving Granville Island into Sunday's event crowd.

Budget — Every named attraction is free. Pay only for transit and food; the False Creek ferry, aquarium, rentals, and market purchases are optional.

July 21 rendezvous — Zhuo Han and Jun Min land at YVR at 6:50 AM. They should take a taxi or rideshare with luggage directly to Enterprise at 10-970 SW Marine Drive and coordinate the meeting before the 9:00 AM handover.

Rental counter — Pack passports, licences, rental confirmation, payment card, chargers, water, breakfast, and road snacks on Monday. Everyone who may drive should attend with a valid licence.
